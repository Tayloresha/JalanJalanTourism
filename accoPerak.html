CLICK TO KNOW MORE ABOUT WEIL HOTEL IPOH
WEIL Hotel Ipoh welcomes guests with an outdoor swimming pool, a buffet restaurant and free WiFi access. Situated in Ipoh Malaysia, it offers stylish guestrooms featuring fully carpeted floors Rooms are elegantly furnished and air-conditioned. Amenities include tea/coffee making facilities, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a minibar and a dining area. The modern en suite bathroom comes with a bathtub and heated showers. Guests can approach the 24-hour Concierge Counter where staff will assist with checking in services. Luggage storage is also available. An array of Malaysian-style cuisines and popular Western dishes will be served at the on-site restaurant. Alternatively, room service is available and breakfast can be enjoyed in the room upon request.
