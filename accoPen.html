CLICK TO KNOW MORE ABOUT SUMMER TREE HOTEL
Featuring modern, air-conditioned guest rooms and a shared lounge, Summer Tree Hotel Penang offers accommodation in George Town. 1.1 km from the property and for guests who drive, free parking is provided. Every room includes parquet flooring and a flat-screen cable TV. The suite bathroom is fitted with a shower and free toiletries. Guests enjoy free WiFi and city view from the room Dining options are plenty around the area, ranging from Vegetarian cuisines to Peranakan dishes. The restaurants are within a 2-minute walk away. Staff at the 24-hour desk can assist you with tour inquiries and daily newspaper is available at the reception. You can spend the evening at the shared lounge, which includes a variety of board games and magazines. Self-service laundry can be operated using small changes, while daily housekeeping is free. Penang Times Square is 800 m from Summer Tree Hotel Penang and Chowrasta Market is 1 km away. The nearest airport is Penang International Airport, 19 km from the property.
