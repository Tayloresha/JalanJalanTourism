ANJUNG PATIN VILLE

Traditional Malay cuisine, served in the heart of Kuala Lumpur.


❮ ❯

Description

In for a comforting traditional Malay cuisine in the bustling city of Kuala Lumpur? Head over to Kampung Baru where as the name implies, it is a haven for Malay cuisine lovers. Among the many restaurants, Anjung Patin Ville is one that stands out for its specialty in serving the best 'ikan patik tempoyak'! Standing magnificently in the premises of an old Malay house in Kampung Baru, with the backdrop of skyscrapers piercing the heart of Kuala Lumpur, Anjung Patin Ville serves as an escape from the hustle of the city.

DAMASCUS BUKIT BINTANG

Middle Eastern favourites right here in Malaysia.


❮ ❯

Description

Now there is no need to travel to the Middle East to taste their cuisine as we have our very own port in Bukit Bintang. Relish in their boneless chicken where the meat is cooked close to the flames taking on a crisp char and succulent texture. The chicken and beef kebabs are one of a kind, moist and huge in portions. Dining in Damascus is incomplete without their Shish Tawook and Lamb Kebab. Dishes meant to be shared and enjoyed with friends and family. Drop by Damascus BBQ Bukit Bintang to have a taste.

LISETTE'S CAFE

Good food complemented with a cozy atmosphere.


❮ ❯

Description

Tucked away in Jalan Kemuja, Lisette's appear almost hidden to passersby in broad daylight with its tranquil exterior. Once you step in, you'd realise how it became an instant favourite to the neighbourhood and KLites in general. From their seasonal beautiful buffets to the everyday menu, they will bring everyone to the same table. Wholesome and nutritious food is certainly a priority here, but for those looking to indulge your sweet tooth, let us assure you that there is plenty to choose from.
