CLICK TO KNOW MORE ABOUT THE PALTINUM 2 KUALA LUMPUR BY LUMA
Located in Kuala Lumpur, 1.2 km from Petronas Twin Towers and 2 km from the centre, The Platinum 2 Kuala Lumpur by LUMA provides air-conditioned accommodation with free WiFi, and an outdoor swimming pool. All units come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with free toiletries, bidet and shower. Some units also have a kitchen equipped with a fridge, a microwave, and a stovetop. A car rental service is available at the aparthotel. Popular points of interest near The Platinum 2 Kuala Lumpur by LUMA include Suria KLCC, Kuala Lumpur Convention Center and KLCC Park. The nearest airport is Sultan Abdul Aziz Shah, 23 km from the accommodation, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
