D'PLACE KINABALU

Kadazandusun delicacies that will leave you thrilled for more.


❮ ❯

Description

If you are craving for a food adventure, D'Place Kinabalu is the place for some serious taste thrill. Featuring bamboo walls and waiters in full Kadazandusun gear, the restaurant serves authentic Kadazandusun delicacies on huge woven platters lined with banana leaves. Their Hinava, a salad of raw fish pickled in lime, shallots, chilli, and ginger is a must-try. The taste of this dish is one that you wouldn't want to miss. Don't forget to try their 'Butod' or sago worms. They are best-sellers for a good reason!

KAMPUNG NELAYAN SEAFOOD

Perfect spot for the die-hard seafood fans.


❮ ❯

Description

A trip to Kota Kinabalu is never complete without having some good seafood. There are many good and affordable seafood restaurants in Kota Kinabalu and Kampung Nelayan Floating Seafood Market Restaurant is one of them. It's the only lakeside floating seafood restaurant in town! Other than serving fresh delicious seafood, they also have Sabah cultural dance performances on Friday, Saturday and Sunday! Be sure to reserve a table to avoid any disappointments!
