NASI DAGANG ATAS TOL

A classic East Coast favourite you'll find nowhere else.


❮ ❯

Description

Nasi dagang is known as the nasi lemak of the east coast and is a breakfast favourite. This Terengganu food is made from a mixture of white glutinous rice, steamed in coconut milk and served with fragrant tuna curry, pickled cucumber, and carrots. The price will not burn a hole in your pocket as good food does not know capitalism. Food prepared by village folks will never go wrong! You might encounter a queue but fret not, they will serve you in a blink of an eye.

WARUNG POK NONG

For the die-hard fans of fresh seafood.


❮ ❯

Description

'Celup Tepung' is a local delicacy that everyone should at least try once when they visit Terengganu. Various fresh seafood are dipped in a batter and fried to perfection. It may sound unpalatable at first, but one bite will negate all your negative assumptions. The crunch from the seafood being perfectly fried, dipped in homemade chilli sauce will make you unconciously order for more. With its strategic spot right beside the beach that faces the South China Sea, visitors can enjoy the cooling breeze while enjoying the fresh seafood.

DAPO PATA KONTENA

Indulge in good food while enjoying Terengganu's night breeze.


❮ ❯

Description

From containers into a food junction place, Dapo Pata Kontena is good for you and your friends to enjoy the night beach breeze and beautiful scenery to feast your eyes while eating your foods. As Terengganu is surrounded by stunning beaches, the breeze it brings is certainly delightful to feel especially at night.Aside from that, live bands, artists, and even magicians are also available to entertain you. There's nothing better than good food, good company and more importantly, a good atmosphere!
